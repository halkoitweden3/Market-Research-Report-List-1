<p><p>The Medical 3D Bioprinting market encompasses various printing technologies: </p><p>1. **Stereolithography (SLA)** utilizes UV light to cure resin layer by layer, offering high precision and detail for intricate structures.</p><p>2. **Fused Deposition Modeling (FDM)** layers thermoplastic materials, suitable for simpler, sturdy designs but with less detail.</p><p>3. **Laser Sintering (LOM)** bonds powder layers using lasers, enabling complex geometries and strong mechanical properties.</p><p>4. **Others** include various specialized methods like inkjet printing and digital light processing, expanding the applications in tissue engineering and regenerative medicine.</p></p>
