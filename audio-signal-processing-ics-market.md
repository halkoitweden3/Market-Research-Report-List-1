<p><p>The audio signal processing IC market is divided into two main types: analog signal processing ICs and digital signal processing ICs. Analog signal processing ICs handle continuous signals, providing functions such as amplification and filtering, crucial in traditional audio applications. In contrast, digital signal processing ICs convert audio signals into digital form for processing, enabling advanced features like noise reduction and audio effects. Both types play vital roles in enhancing audio quality in devices like smartphones, speakers, and professional sound systems.</p></p>
