<p><strong>The White Light Lnterferenc Microscope Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Diffraction Grating Interferometers</li><li>Vertical Scanning or Coherence Probe Interferometers</li><li>White Light Scatter-plate Interferometers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The white light interference microscope market includes several key types: </p><p>1. **Diffraction Grating Interferometers** utilize grating patterns to split light beams, enhancing resolution for surface analysis. </p><p>2. **Vertical Scanning or Coherence Probe Interferometers** employ vertical movement to achieve depth profiling, allowing for detailed 3D imaging of samples. </p><p>3. **White Light Scatter-plate Interferometers** focus on light scattering techniques to analyze surface topology, providing insights into fine structural features. Each type serves specific applications in material science and biology.</p></p>
