<p><p>The stepped variable attenuators market comprises two primary types: manually variable attenuators and continuously variable attenuators. Manually variable attenuators utilize discrete settings or steps, allowing users to adjust signal strength in defined increments, making them suitable for applications requiring specific attenuation levels. In contrast, continuously variable attenuators offer smooth and seamless adjustments of attenuation levels across a wide range, providing greater flexibility and precision in signal management. Both types cater to diverse applications in telecommunications, audio, and instrumentation.</p></p>
