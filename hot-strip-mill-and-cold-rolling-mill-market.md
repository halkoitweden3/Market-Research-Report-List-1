<p><p>The Hot Strip Mill and Cold Rolling Mill markets feature distinct rolling mill types tailored for various steel processing needs. Six-High Rolling Mills introduce two additional rolls for improved flatness and thickness precision, making them suitable for high-quality or thin materials. Each type caters to specific production requirements and material properties.</p></p>
