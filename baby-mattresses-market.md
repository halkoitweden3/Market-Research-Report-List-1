<p><p>The baby mattresses market primarily features two main types: foam and innerspring. Foam mattresses are made from materials like memory foam or polyurethane, offering contouring support and pressure relief, ideal for infants. They are typically lighter and easier to handle. Innerspring mattresses consist of a support core of metal coils, providing durability and breathability. These mattresses often feature added padding for comfort, catering to parents who prefer a traditional feel. Each type offers distinct benefits to meet varying preferences.</p></p>
