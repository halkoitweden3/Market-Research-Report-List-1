<p><p>The built-in electric curtains market can be categorized into three types: automatic, semi-automatic, and manual. Automatic type curtains operate with motorized systems, allowing for remote control or smart home integration for effortless opening and closing. Semi-automatic type curtains offer a mix of manual and automated features, enabling users to use a control mechanism while retaining some manual operation. Manual type curtains rely entirely on physical intervention, requiring users to manually draw the drapes, providing a more traditional and cost-effective option.</p></p>
